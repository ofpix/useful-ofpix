和许多的摄影师一样，在我的心中，“家乡”是一个永远也拍不到尽头，永恒绵长的主题。
新疆是我的故乡，这块古丝绸之路上的主要通道自古都演绎着传奇，充满着太多难以涵盖的政治因素和历史的巧合。于是，试图还原这块土地的真实，往往超越惯有平面摄影语言所能表述的范围，正因为如此，这种复杂性才让新疆的影像长期以来被人云亦云的一张张美丽风景照片所含混地遮盖了。而我选择关注人，人是历史的创造者，同样，历史又将人的定义不断丰富，努力真实记录维吾尔族的影像，把他们从纸片化的、概念化的固有思维里释放出来。
这组照片所展现的是一群生活在城市中的维吾尔族人们，他们从传统的古镇居民转变为城市中的主人，从抽象的画册中走出来，转变为一个多样立体的民族，有别于盘亘在内地人们头脑中的想象和猜测，而这便是我努力走进并了解维吾尔族家庭的初衷，因为只有这样才能打破长期的禁锢和偏见，揭示一份真实。这个摄影项目从新疆的克拉玛依市开始，一直延续到了北京，穿越漫长的路途，试图比较他们在家乡和异乡的民族文化传承。
感谢照片中的维吾尔族家庭，他们的真诚与善良生动演绎出了这组照片的标题——美丽的家，美丽的民族。
“Kara-Beauty ”
Like most other photographers, “Hometown” is an eternal photographic theme in our hearts.
Xinjiang is my hometown. There were so many legends on the trade route of ancient Silk Road, which also filled with the collision of political and coincidences of history. So I think it is impossible to show the original state of this land by the image of traditional photography. And that caused the true image of Xinjiang has been cover with the scene of unusual beauty photographic works. But I concentrate on people, the History was creating by them, and it also enriches the definitions of masses people. Record the real image of Uygur, giving them a relief from the conceptual thought, has performed as the responsibility of a Uygur Photographer.
This is the series of shots I will use to show how Uygur people shift their life from a traditional town to a large, modern city. The purpose I walk into their family is highlight the diversified culture of Xinjiang, to remove the long-term imprisons and bias in the mainland’s people thought. This exhibition starts from Karamay city of Xinjiang, ends in Beijing city, to tell the difference on the transference of Uygur Traditional Culture in the hometown and other cities.
I must give these Uygur families my heartiest thanks, and their sincerity and kindness will perfectly interpret the title of shots – My beautiful hometown, the beautiful nation.
